About Me

Hi, I'm Stephanie!

I am a recent graduate from UMass Amherst, where I studied English with specializations in Professional Writing and Technical Communication & the Study and Practice of Writing.

I currently hold two positions: one with UMass IT Communications, where I'm a Content Coordinator and assist with technical writing and documentation; and the other with the UMass International Programs Office, where I'm a Communications Coordinator. During my time at UMass, I was a peer tutor in the UMass Writing Center and worked as a fiction editor on Jabberwocky, UMass's official undergraduate literary journal.

In my free time, I enjoy creative writing, though that mostly involves sitting in front of a blank page and waiting for my novels to write themselves. I'm also an avid reader, if by avid I mean I love buying new books, only to continue rereading my favorites over and over again.

After graduation, I moved back to my hometown of Silver Spring, Maryland, where I spend most of my time complaining about the humidity and dousing my food in Old Bay.
