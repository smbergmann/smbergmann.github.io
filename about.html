About Me

Hi, I'm Stephanie!

I'm a technical writer currently working as a Knowledge Solutions Specialist with Ticketmaster, where I help manage a library of over 2000 knowledge articles for users of Ticketmaster's products. Prior to that, I worked for F5's App Infrastructure Protection (AIP) product group as a Technical Writer. There, I managed all user interface documentation, API documentation, and release notes for the work of four 10-15 person software engineering teams.

In 2021, I graduated from UMass Amherst with a Bachelor of Arts in English and certificates in Professional Writing and Technical Communication (PWTC) and The Study and Practice of Writing (SPOW).

In my free time, I enjoy creative writing, though that mostly involves sitting in front of a blank page and waiting for my novels to write themselves. I'm also an avid reader, and love swimming and going to concerts.

I currently live in Silver Spring, Maryland, where I spend most of my time complaining about the humidity and dousing my food in Old Bay.
