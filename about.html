About Me

Hi, I'm Stephanie!

I'm passionate about comprehensive, user-friendly documentation, clear and effective communication, and the Oxford/serial comma.

I am currently a Senior Technical Writer at Method, working as a consultant for our largest client. There, I manage integration documentation for the work of two 10-15 person Agile software engineering teams.

My previous work experience includes documentation- and writing-related roles at Ticketmaster, F5, and UMass Amherst's IT Communications Department and International Programs Office.

In 2021, I graduated from UMass Amherst with a Bachelor of Arts in English and certificates in Professional Writing and Technical Communication (PWTC) and The Study and Practice of Writing (SPOW).

The majority of my free time is devoted to creative writing endeavors, though that usually involes sitting in front of a blank page and waiting for my novels to write themselves. I'm also an avid reader, baker, and concert-goer. I currently live in Silver Spring, Maryland, where I spend the remainder of my free time complaining about the humidity and dousing my food in Old Bay.
